Puppy University Package

The perfect package to ensure your pet gets the right start in life by introducing them to the grooming salon environment. Their training in learning how to behave while being groomed is just as important as house training and social skills. This package has been designed to help keep your dog's coat in perfect condition as well as to save you money.

ENROL FOR ¬£120

Four introductory sessions to grooming, building from socialisation and nail clipping right through to their first full groom. Puppies need to be under five months of age and have had all their vaccinations. The package will need to be completed within three months.

From Enrolment to Graduation...

Steps 1 to 3: Three sessions to include socialisation, bath, dry, comb out, face, feet, bottom trim and nail clipping, working towards the final stage.

Step 4: Socialisation, full groom including bath, all over trim and nail clipping.

As well as all this, you will get...
  • A welcome pack with advice, treats, etc.
  • Advice on looking after your dog's coat.
  • A five minute training session on brushing/combing.
  • A five minute 'Cleany Teeth' ultrasound session.
  • A certificate at the end of the course.
  • A graduation photo for mummy and daddy!
